A Small Glimpse into the World of Carla Kroll

Born and raised in Illinois, I am an avid Chicago sports fan. Chicago Cubs, Bears, Bull, and Blackhawks - in that order. Watching the Cubs finally win a World Series was an amazing moment that I'll never forget.

I spent 6 years in the USAF as an Aerospace Ground Equipment mechanic. The military really helped develop me into the person I am today. The military gave me discipline, a desire to grow, and the perseverence to not give up.

When I first separated from the military, I began going to school and did get my Associates degree for desktop publishing. After a few years of working, it was made clear to me that if I wanted to grow in this field, I needed to develop skills for coding. I have been working with HTML and CSS for quite a while now. I have also learned how to use and modify some CMS applications, such as WordPress and Joomla. Although I do not yet have the skills to build the templates from scratch, I have often been tasked with customizing or modifying existing templates.

I really enjoy working with code and I love that it is always growing and expanding. Continuing education is very important to me, and in this field, there is never a lack of information to be absorbed.

I am an animal lover. I currently have 2 rescue dogs that I love so much!
Tucker, my very first dog as an adult, is a 3 year old German Shepherd/Cattle Dog mix.

Lucy is a 2 year old pocket pit that loves snuggles and giving kisses more than any dog I've ever met.

Besides writing code for a living, being involved in animal rescue has become a very important part of my life. I try to fundraise for rescues that are important to me, and volunteer at local shelters on occassion.
